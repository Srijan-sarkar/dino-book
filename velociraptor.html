Velociraptor

This page is about the Velociraptor a carnivorous dinosaur which lived in the creatacous time period. 

              

                  

                      Velociraptor is a theropod dinosaur(a dinosaur which has 3 toes and finger)
                      which lived in the creatacous time period. It mostly fed on small animals but
                      sometimes could take down larger ones too.
                      
                      It could reach speeds upto 40 miles an hour
